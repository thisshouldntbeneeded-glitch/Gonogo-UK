Choose the metric that matches the question

The fastest way to waste survey budget is to ask the wrong question. This section makes the difference obvious in seconds.

NPS = loyalty

Use NPS when leadership wants a wider read on advocacy, retention direction and overall brand strength.

CSAT = touchpoint quality

Use CSAT when you need a score on a specific interaction and want immediate operational insight.

CES = friction

Use CES when the real issue is effort, complexity, confusion or process pain rather than general satisfaction.
