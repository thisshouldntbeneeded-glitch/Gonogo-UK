We use your personal data only where we have a lawful basis under UK GDPR:[web:119][web:118]

  • To protect our rights and the rights of others — including detecting abuse, fraud, or security incidents, based on our legitimate interests in running a safe and lawful service.

Some of our service providers are located outside the UK. 9. How long we keep your data

We keep personal data only for as long as is reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this policy, or to meet legal, accounting or reporting requirements.[web:120]

In practice, this typically means:

  • contact enquiries — usually up to 24 months after we close the enquiry, unless further retention is needed;
  • competition or survey entries — for the duration of the activity and any reasonable follow‑up period;
  • analytics data — aggregated or anonymised as soon as reasonably possible, with identifiable logs kept only for short operational periods.

10. Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the following rights in relation to your personal data, subject to certain conditions:[web:124][web:118]

  • Right of access — to request a copy of your personal data.
  • Right to rectification — to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to erasure — to ask us to delete your data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to restriction — to ask us to limit how we use your data in certain cases.
  • Right to object — to object to processing based on legitimate interests or to direct marketing.
  • Right to data portability — to receive certain data in a structured, commonly used format.
  • Right to withdraw consent — where we rely on your consent, you can withdraw it at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details above. We may need to verify your identity before responding.[web:124]

11. Data security

We use appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal data against accidental or unlawful destruction, loss, alteration, unauthorised disclosure or access. These measures are reviewed periodically in light of industry practice and regulatory guidance.[web:120][web:122]

This website is aimed at adults. We do not knowingly collect personal data relating to children under 13 years old. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can delete it where appropriate.
