How We Score

A transparent 100-point framework.

Every brand is evaluated using a structured scoring model designed to make comparisons clearer, fairer, and easier to trust.

Compliance & Regulation
Licensing, regulatory standing, industry body memberships, and legal compliance within the UK market.
Customer Satisfaction
Real user feedback, review signals, complaint trends, and overall customer sentiment.
Product Value
Pricing fairness, feature depth, fee transparency, and value for money.
Innovation
Technology adoption, digital experience, differentiation, and forward-thinking delivery.
Customer Support
Support access, responsiveness, issue resolution quality, and ease of getting help.
Accessibility & Security
Platform accessibility, security standards, privacy practices, and inclusive design.
