Survey software questions, answered

Everything teams usually ask before they pick a feedback tool.

How much does GoNoGo Surveys cost in the UK?

GoNoGo Surveys is £40 per month, billed monthly, with no setup fee. There are no per-response charges and no per-seat upsell. Cancel any time.

Can I send NPS and CSAT surveys with GoNoGo?

Yes. The builder ships with a dedicated NPS scale, a CSAT smiley question type, and all the supporting questions you need to run a full customer experience programme — including single-select, multi-select, dropdown, yes/no, short text, long text, info screens and consent.

Does it support branching and skip logic?

Yes. Any question can route respondents to another question, end the survey early, or branch into a follow-up — based on operators like equals, contains, greater than, less than, answered and not answered.

Can I distribute surveys with a QR code?

Every survey gets a unique link and a printable QR code. Drop the QR onto receipts, table talkers, in-store signage, vehicles or product packaging, and feedback flows straight into your dashboard.

Is it mobile-friendly?

Yes. Surveys are mobile-first and render in two modes: a classic single-page flow for considered feedback, and a swipe-based party mode designed for very high completion rates on phones.

Where is my survey data stored, and is it UK GDPR-friendly?

All responses are stored securely on encrypted infrastructure with strict access controls. There is a built-in consent question type, you own your data, and you can export or delete responses at any time — all aligned with UK GDPR principles.

Do I need a developer to set this up?

No. If you can use Google Docs, you can build a GoNoGo survey. There is no code, no integration work, and no waiting on IT. You can launch a survey in under five minutes.

Can I use this without the full GoNoGo Business portal?

Yes. GoNoGo Surveys is a standalone product. You do not need to be on the full Business plan to use the survey builder, dashboard and distribution tools.
